1. Conditional Statements

Key Concept

Conditional statements are fundamental in AI for decision making and pattern recognition. They allow your program to make choices based on data.

# Example: AI Decision Making confidence_score = 0.85 threshold = 0.7 if confidence_score > threshold: prediction = "Positive Class" action = "Accept prediction" else: prediction = "Uncertain" action = "Request human review" print(f"Prediction: {prediction}") print(f"Action: {action}")
